Which is My Preferred school board?
A
Your input zone is the key to your most preferred learning method. Different school boards prefer different teaching methods emphasizing on- Text, Pictures, Audio, Visuals, Demonstrations, practical, Hands-on-Activities etc. Check out your most preferred school board.
SSC
(SECONDARY SCHOOL CERTIFICATE)
Provided & governed by state government. Every State has its own education board which looks after the academic affairs of the schools coming under it. Moderately loaded curriculum stressing on Text & Picture based teaching. Recommended for kids with stronger visual input zone
