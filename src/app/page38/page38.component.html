GROSS MOTOR SKILLS (Body Smart)
Gross Motor Skills (GMS) is the co-ordination of large muscular movements of the body in synchronization with the thought process. People with high GMS are very swift in moving and performing action. They are very active physically as well as mentally. They tend to be pro-active towards kinesthetic challenges which involve physical action. They are experienced based learners and understand better by exploring pragmatically.
GMS Defines Profoundness
of Pro-activity of a Person
  • Big muscular movements
  • Running, Jumping, Walking etc.
  • All Physical activities
  • Strength & Stamina
  • Pro-activeness & Endurance
  • Rhythmic movements & Co-ordination
  • Outdoor & All field & track games
  • Grabbing Opportunities
CHARACTERSTICS OF OF PERSON HAVING HIGH GROSS MOTORS SKILL
PROS
  • Can excel in Sports: All track & Field sports,
    Boxing, Martial arts, Swimming, etc.
  • Can excel in Music: Playing of all Gross
    instruments e.g. Drums, Dhol etc.
  • Can excel in Performing Arts: Different Dance
    forms, Action Films, Reality Shows etc.
  • Good at Operations, Grabbing opportunities &
    Adventure activities. Always on the go.
CONS
  • Need constant mobility for paying attention or to
    concentrate. Brain sleeps when body is not mobile.
  • Constantly moving legs, changing siting
    positions or causing distractions in meetings or lectures.
  • Often very hyperactive. May leave tasks halfway,
    frequently change mind or cut-off relations in haste.
  • Good at physical activity but still requires equal
    contributions from other intelligences to perform.

1) FIND A WAY TO INDULGE INTO ROUTINE PHYSICAL TASKS.
  • Morning walk (Brisk walking) or jogging.
  • Join Gymnasium or hire a professional trainer.
  • Join Yoga/ karate/ Swimming classes.
2) PLAY OUTDOORS SPORTS WHENEVER YOU FIND TIME, ESPECIALLY AT WEEKENDS.
