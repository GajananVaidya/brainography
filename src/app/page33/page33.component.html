INTRAPERSONAL (Self-Smart)
Intrapersonal means "within the self—so, Intrapersonal intelligence is another term for self-awareness or introspection. People who have high Intrapersonal intelligence are more aware of their emotions, motivations, beliefs, goals, limitations and fears. They have better clarity on "what I like", "what I dislike", "who I am", and "what I want'. Although they are introvert by nature and prefer to work alone, they can also be good at socializing when required.

CHARACTERISTICS OF PERSON HAVING HIGH INTRAPERSONAL INTELLIGENCE
PROS
  • Self-disciplined, Responsible and good in time
    Management.
  • Self-directed, learns from his/her Failures and
    Successes.
  • Good in Planning, Execution, Administration and
    Management.
  • Rational and judicious thinker, high on
    Self-reflection.
CONS
  • Cannot handle — Criticism, Comparison and
    Direct orders (need others to request).
  • Need self-respect (egoistic) and Privacy
    (need others to respect their privacy).
  • Prefer one to one Interaction, have difficulties
    working in team. Highly Independent.
  • Needs lot of Appreciation, Reciprocation and
    Acknowledgement.
REMEDIAL MEASURES IF YOUR INTRAPERSONAL INTELLIGENCE IS LOW
Take measures to improve the following
1)  YOUR SELF-RESPECT
  • Don't let others to take you for granted. Avoid activities that can bring disgrace to your image. Get yourself counted for good reasons.
2)  YOUR SELF-BELIEF AND CONFIDENCE
  • Read /watch motivational books and videos, say loudly "I can do it". Boost your self- esteem. Avoid company of losers.
3)YOUR DISCIPLINE
  • Work on your Personality development — set short term and long term goals, follow timetables and to-do lists strictly.
4)  YOUR RATIONAL THINKING
  • Be Honest with yourself and Reasonable with others.
5)  YOUR IDENTITY WITH YOURSELF
  • Spend time with yourself, Play target based games, develop a hobby, attend courses and tests to explore your Potential.
