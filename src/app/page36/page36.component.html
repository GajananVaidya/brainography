REMEDIAL MEASURES IF YOUR 3D- SPATIAL INTELLIGENCE IS LOW
Take measures to improve the following
1)  PERSISTENCE AND PERSEVERANCE-
  • First step towards improving your 3D-Spatial is to gather will-power and strong determination because you will be required to
    train your guns on connecting the threads in your mind, which requires serious concentration and causes mental exhaustion.
2)  JOIN BRAIN GYM
  • Explore Spatial Puzzles and Games, Learn Rubik cubes, try drawing and Painting, Play Action/ Thriller Video Games.
  • Take extra courses in Chemistry, Trigonometry, Calculus and Comprehension.
  • Develop hobbies like Videography, Flying Drones, Bartending, Astrology, etc. Also learn planning in context to future.
  • Explore YouTube channels on Cosmology, Architecture, Life planning etc.
3)  EXPLORE PHYSICAL WORLD.
  • Explore real world and train yourself to visualize Space, Distance and Direction. Observe closely-Roads, Bridges, Cities,
    Mountains, Oceans, etc. Develop sense of distance without having to measure it. Also by observing Orientation of things
    practically improve on your Memory by Reference.
4)  IMPROVE MEMORY RECALL
  • Do Meditation on regular basis and improve on your concentration.
  • Include Memory Enhancing Diet and Supplements. Check with Nutritionist & find what is suitable for you
  • Do regular Physical Exercise and Sleep well to enhance Memory Consolidation Process.
