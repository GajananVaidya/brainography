MUSIC & EMOTIONS (Music Smart)
Musical & Emotional intelligence is sensitivity and understanding of sounds which generate sensations and feelings within oneself. People with high musical intelligence are attracted to music by default and they are very sensible towards melodies, rhythms, tunes, tones, pitch, meter ,tempo, and timbre. They can develop 'Absolute Pitch' to be able to explore music. They are also highly emotional persons by default.
Musical Intelligence is Relating
Sounds with Feelings.
  • Sound Sensitivity
  • Sound pattern & Volume
  • Love & Urge for Music
  • Melodies, Tune, Rhythm & Tempo
  • Frequency, Pitch, Tone & Timbre
  • Feelings & Emotions
  • Non-verbal communication
  • Sympathy & Compassion
CHARACTERISTICS OF PERSON WITH HIGH MUSICIAL & EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE
PROS
  • Aptitude for understanding Rhythms, Patterns &
    Music as a Whole.
  • Good in either playing/performing, composing or
    appreciating different forms of music.
  • Always thinks of doing good to others.
    Zeal for Social- Service.
  • Highly Emotional & Sympathetic. Connects by
    Heart & communicates through feelings.
CONS
  • Feels uneasy or mentally disturbed if neglects
    music for longer duration. Music is unavoidable.
  • Vulnerable to manipulation or exploitation due to
    altruistic nature. May be also taken for granted.
  • Takes things too personal. At times disconnects with
    outer world when emotionally disturbed or hurt.
  • Makes personal sacrifices for others.
REMEDIAL MEASURES IF YOUR MUSICIAL- EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE IS LOW
Take measures to improve the following
1) IMPROVE YOUR TOLERANCE
  • Listen to unfamiliar style of music even if you don't like it, twice a week. Try to develop your taste for it.
  • Engage yourself frequently with people whom you don't like, without getting irritated.
  • Appreciate atleast 5 people everyday
2) LEARN TO EXPRESS SYMPATHY TOWARDS OTHERS
  • Give others a chance to talk about their emotions. Listen first, offer comments later. Avoid invalidating other person's feelings.
  • Express support and use appropriate physical contact.
3) DEVELOP A MUSICAL HOBBY
  • Learn at least one Musical instrument and practice religiously or join singing classes. Practice with group of people or
    Karaoke tracks.
4) INDULGE INTO SOCIAL WORKS
  • Visit Orphanage frequently or Help Physically/ Mentally challenged & elderly people. Join NGO's, Trusts or Organizations
    related to Social Cause and Charity.
