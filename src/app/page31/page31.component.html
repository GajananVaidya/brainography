How do I tend to take in the information?
FACT
Fact-based people are all about what they can rationalize, understand,
measure & prove. Pay attention to physical reality.
Influenced by information, based on real occurrences which can be
verified & supported by proofs, statistics, documentation, etc., and
can be tested by concrete evidence. Looks for actual, present & real.
OPINION
Opinion-based people are all about feeling & believing in doing what
intuitively seems right to them. Pay attention to impressions & patterns.
Influenced by feelings, thoughts, perceptions, desires, attitude,
experiences, understandings, beliefs, values, etc., which cannot be
tested by concrete evidence. Looks for patterns or abstract theories.

How do I tend to make my decisions?
OBJECTIVE
Put more weight on objective principles & impersonal facts.
Like to find the basic truth or principle to be applied, regardless of the
specific situation involved. Focuses on inconsistencies.
Analyze pros & cons, and then be consistent & logical in deciding.
Not influenced by personal wishes or other people's wishes.
SUBJECTIVE
Put more weight on personal concerns and the people involved.
Consider what people care about & the points-of-view of persons
involved in a situation. Focuses on communications.
Concerned with values and what is the best for the people involved.
Appear caring, warm, & tactful, maintaining harmony in relationships.
