LINGUISTIC - VERBAL (Language Smart)
Linguistic - Verbal Intelligence is ability to understand Spoken & Written language and propensity to deliver it efficiently by Speaking or Writing. People with high linguistic intelligence are prolific in verbal as well as written communication. They are naturally good at sensing the Meanings, Synonyms, Antonyms, Tenses, Plurals, Acronyms, Metaphors, Phrases and Jargons of words or sentences.
Linguistics defines Expertise
in Correspondence
  • Language learning ability
  • Hearing identification
  • Attention to Word & Vocabulary
  • Attention to Meaning & Understanding
  • Verbal Communication
  • Speech, Pronunciation & Accent
  • Paraphrasing, Comprehension, Spellings
  • Short term memory
CHARACTERISTICS OF A PERSON HAVING HIGH LINGUISTIC-VERBAL INTELLIGENCE
PROS
  • Enjoy reading & writing, excellent in Word Puzzles,
    have a knack of Picking up new Languages
  • Expressing creativity in language, both verbally or
    written word. Rich Vocabulary
  • Ability to Savor convoluted words, Persuade,
    Negotiate & Communicate complex concepts
  • Excellent Short term memory, Handy for speech,
    Presentation, Anchoring etc
CONS
  • Becomes habitual in using exclusive and
    exorbitant words even when not applicable
  • Develops unique accent which later becomes
    the identity of the person
  • At times keeps pointing out mistakes in
    languages of others
  • May do excessive talking.
    (if extrovert by nature)
REMEDIAL MEASURES IF YOUR LINGUISTIC-VERBAL INTELLIGENCE IS LOW
Take measures to improve the following
1) WORD MEMORY IS LIKE A MUSCLE. YOU CAN TRAIN IT TO BE STRONGER.
  • Develop reading & writing habits
  • Read newspapers completely. Mark difficult words and then find their meanings & try to use them.
  • Join a library or buy books (or E-books). Select different subjects each time
  • Write at least 1 page of events of the entire day on daily bases. Maintain a dairy.
2) LEARN A NEW WORD EVERY DAY.
  • Use Thesaurus on Google for improving vocabulary. Develop a habit of searching Synonyms & Acronyms of words. Practice
    difficult Pronunciations by repeating it several times & Spellings by writing them several times.
3) SOLVE CROSSWORDS AND WORD PUZZLES.
  • Play Scrabble & similar word games. Participate in Spelling, elocution, Story-telling, Essay & Speed reading contests.
4) JOIN CLASSES TO LEARN A FOREIGN LANGUAGE.
5) PRACTICE ON SPEECH, JOIN VOICE OVER CLASSES, PRACTICE TONGUE TWISTERS.
