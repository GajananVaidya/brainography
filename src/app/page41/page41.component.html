NATURALIST-MINUTE OBSERVATION (Nature Smart)
Naturalist Intelligence is the ability to observe minutely the gradual changes in Nature. In other words, the ability to utilize the 5 senses efficiently to recognize Flora and Fauna and make other Consequential Distinctions in Natural World. People with high Naturalist Intelligence have sharp Observation and affinity towards Nature (Living and Non-living). They are very practical & good at displaying their talents.
Naturalist is Observing Minutely & Bonding with Nature
  • Love for Animals, Plants & Nature
  • Minute Observation & Detailing
  • Visual Identification & Sensitivity
  • Reading, Editing, Auditing, Documenting
  • Identifying errors, Trouble shooting
  • Speed, Judgement & Trajectory
  • Intensity of all 5 Senses
  • Biology & Culinary skills
CHARACTERISTICS OF A PERSON HAVING HIGH NATURALIST INTELLIGENCE
PROS
  • Ability to classify Living & Non- living entities
    Ex. Animals, Plant species, Rocks etc.
  • Prolific in Reading, Editing, Auditing, Identifying
    errors, Speed Judgement & Cooking.

REMEDIAL MEASURES IF YOUR NATURALIST INTELLIGENCE IS LOW
Take measures to improve the following
1) IMPROVE YOUR SENSIBLITY.
  • The most effective way to improve the profoundness of all the 5 Senses is to do Rigorous Meditation. Develop Inquisitiveness
    & try to find out 'Why?' & 'How?' of everything that you don't know.
2) IMPROVE YOUR MINUTE OBSERVATION.
  • Play Games such as Optical Illusion, Spot the Difference, Jigsaw Puzzles etc.
  • Watch plants grow on daily basis and try to observe the gradual changes happening each day.
  • Assign yourself a challenging Scavenger Hunt activity and photo shoot the subject. Explore the details
3) GET CONNECTED TO NATURE.
  • Adopt a Pet Animal and learn to communicate with it.
  • Try Gardening of rare and exotic plants. Grow herbs & spices at home.
  • Make frequent visits to Nature parks, Botanical gardens or zoo.
  • Learn Natural Life Art & Photography. Take part in contests.
  • Use Binoculars, Telescopes, Microscopes & Magnifiers to see things differently.
  • Improve understanding of Nature and Cosmos. Watch Discovery, Animal Planet, National Geographic etc.
  • Indulge in Nature related Sports such as Biking, Trekking, Swimming, Golf etc.
4) LEARN COOKING.
  • Join cooking classes or learn to cooking online from YouTube. Try dishes from various international cuisines.
