Q
How can I advance in my profession?
A
If you keep these 3 parameters in mind you are bound to progress.
1) Type of your Profession- YOUR PROFESSIONAL QUADRANT
2) Type of Job role in that Profession- YOUR FORTE
3) The degree of your Specified Quality- YOUR QUOTIENT
1) YOUR PROFESSIONAL QUADRANTS
Let's first understand the Professional Quadrants & how do you rate in each section. The ultimate reason for being into a profession is to earn your livelihood. Professions are governed by either a 'Set of Rules' or a 'Process' or a 'System', where some people like to invent them and others like to either follow them or use them to their advantage. All the roles are interdependent on each other. Let's identify what suits you better.
