Q
How can I advance in my profession?
A
If you keep these 3 parameters in mind you are bound to progress.
1) Type of your Profession- YOUR PROFESSIONAL QUADRANT
2) Type of Job role in that Profession- YOUR FORTE
3) The degree of your Specified Quality- YOUR QUOTIENT
1) YOUR PROFESSIONAL QUADRANTS
Let's first understand the Professional Quadrants & how do you rate in each section. The ultimate reason for being into a profession is to earn your livelihood. Professions are governed by either a 'Set of Rules' or a 'Process' or a 'System', where some people like to invent them and others like to either follow them or use them to their advantage. All the roles are interdependent on each other. Let's identify what suits you better.

EMPLOYEE
  • An Employee prefers to accomplish a task or manage a program which is pre-decided by of rules, process or a system designed by their Employers.
  • Dynamic performer once the guidelines & targets are understood.
  • Earning are mostly by remunerations, wages, compensations, incentives or sometimes on fixed contracts.
SELF EMPLOYED
  • A Self Employed prefer to work individually for self either by working him/herself or by hiring few employees where the set of rules, process or a system is design by him /herself.
  • Classified as Professionals, Entrepreneurs, Freelancers or Private contractors.
  • Earns from professional fees, net profit or from deals in case of freelancing or private contracts.
INVESTOR
  • An Investors prefers to follow his/her analysis & guts to allocate his/her capital trusting a set of rule, process or a system designed by someone else predicting financial returns.
  • The other 3 quadrants can also be investors along with their current jobs & businesses
  • Earnings are in form of financial returns from investing on stocks, commodities, real estate or various business models.
BUSINESS OWNER
  • A Business Owner prefers to design a set of rules, process or a system to be followed by others rather than by him/herself delegating entire work load to hierarchy of staff and management.
  • Can work individually or in a team where core decisions are taken by him/herself.
  • Earns from the profit or by selling off the entire model to someone.
