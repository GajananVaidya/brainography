PRAGMATISTS can work in various fields, but they feel comfortable working in an organization where structure & hierarchy are well established. PRAGMATISTS are most productive when working by themselves, but they also appreciate the value of working in a team as well, as long as their subordinates render reasonable contribution. They have a very clear command of time & priorities. With all their preconditions, PRAGMATISTS still can be counted among the employees, known for their invaluable & unparalleled contribution. Their core competency lies in planning, organizing & mapping out schedules to follow the agenda
