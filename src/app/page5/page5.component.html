Q
What Is The First Thing I Should Know?
A
Just like a CPU (Central Processing Unit) of a Computer which receives information from keyboard & mouse, our central nervous system (CNS) receives information from our 5 senses. Our CNS is made up of nerve cells which are called Neurons. They are highly specialized cells responsible for communicating information in both chemical and electrical forms. Our brain collects data with the help of Neurons via 6 input zones. The higher the Neural density stronger the zone. So naturally, stronger input zones will become the most preferred input method of the brain by default.
